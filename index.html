Our Story

Jasmine and Ian

Several years ago (2011) we purchased a TheraPlate to help the rehab horses at Traumhof maintain muscle tone and bone density and to help them recover from various injuries. We have been very pleased with the results, not only for these purposes but even more so in other areas. For example, it has helped us resolve many mild colics (and some that appeared quite serious) with little or no vet intervention. It has also helped reduce abcess recurrences in horses with a long history of chronic abcesses, healed hoof granulation where nothing else has helped, and accelerated hoof growth in multiple cases. See our Horse Page for more details on the benefits of the TheraPlate for horses.

Various forms of vibration therapy have well-documented track records, with hundreds of peer-reviewed studies, for helping both humans and animals recover from injuries as well as develop and maintain healthy bodies. We particularly like the TheraPlate's unique 4 Zone Vortex Wave design for its zero impact experience. Under the rubber top there are four separate metal plates, one for each of the horse's legs. Each plate is driven in a planar orbital motion by a separate motor. The controller allows you to vary the frequency of this motion. These fast, tiny motions travel through the entire body, but there is no vertical compression or rocking, so no risk of further injury or aggravating existing conditions.

Lily enjoying the TheraPlate at Traumhof

Whether the horses recognize the benefits or just like how it makes them feel, it's clear that they enjoy using the TheraPlate - they eagerly rush to it, step up onto the plate, and stand happily for the duration of their session. We (and many of our clients, visitors, farriers, etc) also enjoy using the TheraPlate ourselves to help relax sore muscles, tight backs, tired feet, and everything else that comes along with a horse farm!
